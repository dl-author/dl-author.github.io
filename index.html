Molly's Mission is the science fiction sequel to Phase Rider. She is telepathically connected to Bobby because their unleashed minds are operating at superhuman levels. Sine Qua Non is an exploration of the beginning and deterioration of human happiness. About

David Levin was born in the island city of Galveston. He is a Houston attorney whose practice has given him an intimate view of extreme life experiences. He uses this background to explore compelling storylines: life struggling to become free and to survive, people laboring under heart-wrenching despair, the negative side of historic institutions, irrational hatred, the morality of righteous force, and struggles between bipolar interests.

His religious upbringing leads the author to have a sanguine outlook for humankind. He and his wife, Peggy, have two sons, four grandchildren, and sibling wheaten terriers.
